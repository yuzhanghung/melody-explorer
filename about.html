Our Mission

SoftSound was created to help people relax, unwind, and discover calming music. Our goal is to make music discovery simple, peaceful, and enjoyable — whether you need focus, sleep, or a cozy mood.

Key Features

  • Explore songs by mood such as Calm, Chill, Sleep, Focus, and Cozy
  • Listen to 30-second audio previews
  • View album art and artist details
  • Save your favorite tracks
  • Simple, clean design that supports relaxation

Why We Built This

Sometimes music apps feel overwhelming. SoftSound is designed to be a slower, softer alternative — a place where you can find the right sound without distractions. Whether you’re studying, resting, or taking a break, we want this space to feel peaceful.
